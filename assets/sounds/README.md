# Activation Sound Placeholder

This directory should contain the activation sound file: `activation.mp3`

## Creating Your Activation Sound

You can create a custom activation sound using:

1. **Online Tools**:
   - https://ttsmaker.com/ - Text-to-speech (use "Initializing" or a beep sound)
   - https://www.zapsplat.com/ - Free sound effects
   - https://freesound.org/ - Creative commons sounds

2. **Recommended Sound**:
   - Short beep or chime (0.5-1 second)
   - Futuristic/tech sound
   - Clear and distinct
   - Format: MP3, 44.1kHz

3. **Example Sounds**:
   - Quick "beep" sound
   - Sci-fi activation tone
   - "System ready" voice
   - Subtle notification chime

## Temporary Solution

Until you add a custom sound, the app will attempt to play the sound but will gracefully continue if the file is missing.

## Adding Your Sound

1. Create or download an activation sound
2. Name it `activation.mp3`
3. Place it in this directory: `assets/sounds/activation.mp3`
4. The app will automatically use it

## Format Requirements

- **Format**: MP3 or WAV
- **Duration**: 0.5-2 seconds recommended
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- **Bit Rate**: 128kbps or higher
- **Channels**: Mono or Stereo
